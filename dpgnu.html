dPGNU (Google News URL)

Last updated: Wednesday 27 May 2009 15:07

UNIX name Owner Status Version Compatible with
dpgnu Damien Pitard stable 2.0.3 4.0.x - 4.1.x
The "Google News URL" extension makes Google able to index your articles in Google News.

Description

Google News can index your site if the URL for each article contains an unique number consisting of at least three digits (see http://www.google.com/support/new...bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=68323)

This extension uses eZURLAliasFilter mechanism to concat the node_id at the end of your articles URLs.

Note: this extension has been previously published on http://ez.no/developer/contribs/applications/dpgnu_dp_google_news_url
