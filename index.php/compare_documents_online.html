<h2> Compare Documents Online</h2>

GroupDocs Comparison is a web-based app that allows users to compare two version of a document online, right in a web-browser. The app compares uploaded documents automatically and shows all differences by highlighting them with underlines and strikeouts, just like Microsoft Word's change tracking feature does, but online.

With this extension you can easily integrate the GroupDocs Comparison app into your ez Publish website. Once installed, you can invite users to <a href="http://groupdocs.com/apps/comparison" target="_blank">compare documents online</a> right from your website. End-users don't need to download or install any 3rd party plugins or other software - documents can be compared in any modern web-browser, including IE 8+, Firefox, Chrome, Opera and Safari.

<h3>Supported Document Formats</h3>

With the help of this app, users can compare online documents of literally all common business formats, including:

  • Word-processing documents (DOC, DOCX, DOCM, DOT, DOTM, DOTX, TXT, RTF, ODT)
  • Excel spreadsheets (XLS, XLSX, CSV)
  • PowerPoint presentations (PPT, PPTX)
  • HTML documents
  • PDF documents
