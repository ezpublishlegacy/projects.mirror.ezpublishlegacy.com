JW Player

Last updated: Friday 01 August 2008 02:00

UNIX name Owner Status Version Compatible with
all2ejwplayer Max Keil beta 0.0.2 3.x, 4.x
eZ publish integration of the JW player for streaming video.

Description:

The JW FLV Player is an easy and flexible way to add video and audio to a website.

It supports playback of any format the Adobe Flash Player can handle (FLV, MP4, MP3, AAC, JPG, PNG and GIF).

It also supports RTMP, HTTP and live streaming, various playlists and captioning formats, a wide range of settings as well as different video player skins.

The extension contains template operators "jwplayer" and "jwplayerinline" to start up the videostream. You can also use the customtag "jwplayer" in your XML-Block to include the video.

Here are some examples:

  • <a href='javascript:{jwplayer("example.flv","Standard")}'>Stream</a>
  • {jwplayerinline("example.flv", "Inline")}

First parameter: url to your video (required).

Second parameter: jwplayer class defined in the all2ejwplayerclasses.ini (optional).
