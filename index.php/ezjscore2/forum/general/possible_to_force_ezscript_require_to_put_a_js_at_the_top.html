I think he meant modifying the ezjscore_load call to include dojo.js as first param, calling it several times will indeed cause issues.

The cleaneste is to add it to FrontendJavaScriptList/BackendJavaScriptList (first is for frontend and second is for backend) in design.ini to make it load as one of the first scripts on all pages.
