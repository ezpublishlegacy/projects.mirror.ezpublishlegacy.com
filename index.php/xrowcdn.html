xrowCDN ( Content Delivery Network )

Last updated: Thursday 10 September 2009 21:25

UNIX name Owner Status Version Compatible with
xrowcdn Björn Dieding@xrow.de stable N/A > 4.1
xrowCDN connects eZ Publish with a Content Delivery Network

xrowCDN handles the rewriting of any internal URLs to appropiate Locations of the CDN. Futhermore it automates the process of pushing new content to the CDN.

xrowCDN is plug-in based and can potentually connect to any CDN.

It consits out of

  • A shell script and a cronjob to push files to the CDN
  • A Outputfilter for mapping the URLs to the CDN
  • A plug-in for the Cloudfront / S3 Amazon service.
