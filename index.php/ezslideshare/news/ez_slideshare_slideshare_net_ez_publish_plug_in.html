eZ Slideshare, slideshare.net - eZ Publish plug-in

Nicolas Pastorino

Wednesday 02 February 2011 14:04

Simple, yet useful

Simple, yet useful, this extension allows for seamlessly embedding slideshare.net presentations in eZ Publish's content, from an editor's point of view.

Lean more on the project homepage : http://projects.ez.no/ezslideshare

PS : this extension is used on share.ez.no, meaning you can now share your slides through slideshare and embed them in the forums and posts on your blog.
