eZ Authorize

Last updated: Wednesday 30 November 2011 06:00

UNIX name Owner Status Version Compatible with
ezauthorize Brookins Consulting stable 1.4.0 3.5+, 3.6+, 3.7+, 3.8+, 3.9+, 4.x+
eZ Authorize is a complete eZ publish payment gateway extension which provides transparent, secure credit card payment processing using the eZ publish and the Authorize.Net credit card processing service.

eZ Authorize provides a complete and secure Authorize.net payment gateway
extension for eZ Publish 4.x+ and eZ Publish 3.x-;

eZ Authorize the first public release of it's kind in terms of out-of-the-box functionality and extensive high quality documentation.

Audience

We are are working to aid existing and new North American / United States
eZ publish e-commerce customers use eZ publish to power their
e-commerce solutions, getting them online both quickly and efficiently.

Customers need seamless payment solutions for their eZ publish store,
keeping the customer on their application server through the entire
payment and checkout process (re: avoiding Paypal like, site redirects)
which eZ Authorize provides a complete secure solution for users of
Authorize.NET.
