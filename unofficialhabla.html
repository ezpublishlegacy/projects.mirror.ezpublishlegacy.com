A livehelp, instant message, chat solution for web sites

About

A javascript design extension provides unofficial support of the hab.la website chat instant message software.

Note: Hab.la has been reborn as olark.com, we are considering releasing an updated and re-branded extension. Please voice your support for this potential update in the project.ez.,no forums.

What is Hab.la?

Hab.la is a lightweight method to chat with visitors to your website using your existing instant messaging client. Visitors to your website appear as buddies on your Buddy list, their messages to you appear as IMs.

Please review the extension contents to better understand the basis for this solution as a reference.
