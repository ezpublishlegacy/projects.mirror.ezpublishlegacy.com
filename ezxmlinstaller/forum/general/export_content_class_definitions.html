Hi Sebastian,

I had the same question as you, only a few documentation is available and I looked into the source code and tried:

You can export a xml of your content classes and your roles via your administration interface:

http://www.example.com/your_admin_siteaccess/xmlexport/roles and save it as roles.xml into a folder called extension/ezxmlinstaller/data

or

http://www.example.com/your_admin_siteaccess/xmlexport/classes and save it as classes.xml into a folder called extension/ezxmlinstaller/data

You can change your classes or roles very comfortable in a UTF-8 XML editor of your choice. One point is important:
In your classes.xml there is a classExistAction="new" for every content class. I you don't change it, you'll have every content class twice.

classExistAction="replace“ replaces your content class
classExistAction="extend“ extends your content class

to reimport your content classes or for a fresh import use and enjoy:
php extension/ezxmlinstaller/bin/php/xmlinstaller.php --file=extension/ezxmlinstaller/data/classes.xml
