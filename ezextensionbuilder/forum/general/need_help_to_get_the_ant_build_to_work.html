Hi I fixed it by doing this in the build.xml url



 

-<taskdef resource="net/sf/antcontrib/antcontrib.properties"/>


+<taskdef resource="net/sf/antcontrib/antcontrib.properties">


+ <classpath>


+   <pathelement location="/root/.ant/lib/ant-contrib-1.0b3-jar.jar"/>


+ </classpath>


+</taskdef>
