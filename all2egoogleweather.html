Description:

This Extension provides a new Datatype "Google Weather", that allow you to show the weather for the city that you set up. The Datatype is based on the google toolbar weather forecasts.

Installation:

  • copy the extension into your "extension" folder
  • activate it in your site.ini in the override folder
  • create a new class with the new datatype "Google Weather"
  • insert your city and country
