Hi Guillaume,

Thanks for your valueable information.

You will definitly need eZ Publish 4.3 for this to work. Please do not use any alpha or beta of the 4.3 that have been released now.

At the moment your only option is to install it with trunk or the next eZ publish 4.3 beta release.

Concerning this error
/srv/www/htdocs/ezpublish-4.2/lib/ezc/Archive/src/file/block_file.php on line 636

Please remove var/storage/packages and start over. Sometimes it seems eZ doesn`t download that stuff correctly.
