Implementation of sofortüberweisung.de (Payment Network AG) payment gateway

What is "DIRECTebanking.com"?

"DIRECTebanking.com" is a new, innovative payment method with TÜV certificate and transaction security conforming to TÜV standards. DIRECTebanking.com was developed by Payment Network AG and is already being used in numerous online shops in Germany.

Via the secure Payment Network AG payment form which is not accessible to vendors, DIRECTebanking.com activates an automatic remittance in real time from your online bank account. In this way, the purchase amount is transferred instantly and deposited directly into the bank account of the vendor.

This is how "DIRECTebanking.com" works

Individual steps in overview

1. Step: "Remittance form"

First please enter your bank details (account name, account number and bank code), so that we can establish a connection to your bank. The name of your bank will be filled in automatically.
The following information will already be on the remittance form:

  • Remittance amount
  • Purpose (e.g. transfer number, customer number)

2. Step: "Proof of identity"

Please enter the following, as for online banking with your bank:

  • Bank login name (or direct banking number or account number, depending on the bank)
  • Bank PIN

 

3. Step: "Transfer remittance"

Please check all your remittance details once more at this point.

To authorise the remittance, you will need to enter a valid TAN, issued to you by your bank, and then click on the "Confirm remittance and finish order" button.

For more information about the payment gateway visit:
https://www.payment-network.com/
