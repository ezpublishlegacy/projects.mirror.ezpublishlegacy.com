GroupDocs Word & PDF Viewer is a next generation Document Management solution that makes it easier for businesses to collaborate, share and work with documents online. So, organise, view, annotate, compare, assemble and share all your documents and files with eZ Publisher
When you want to add document to website page, just go to Admin > GroupDocs
Presumably you have GroupDocs Account and files in there, just grab File ID from there and paste in the form.

Further versions will offer: Uploading local file from eZ Admin, browsing files from GroupDocs account from your eZ Admin, GroupDocs Annotation with more dynamic functionalities at your document interface and many more.
